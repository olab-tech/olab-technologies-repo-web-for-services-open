5 Shopify SEO Mistakes That Are Costing You Sales

By Olab Technologies Team • October 26, 2023

Every Shopify store owner wants more organic traffic, but many make simple, avoidable mistakes that hold them back. In this article, we'll break down the five most common SEO errors we see and provide actionable steps to fix them today.

1. Ignoring Image Alt Text

This is the most common and easiest mistake to fix. Image alt text tells Google what your product images are about, helping them rank in Google Image Search. Leaving it blank is like having an invisible product.

How to fix it: Go to each product image in your Shopify admin and fill in the "Alt text" field with a clear, descriptive sentence. For example, "Women's black leather ankle boots with side zipper."

2. Duplicate Content from Product Descriptions

Using the exact same manufacturer's description as dozens of other stores means you're competing with them directly with identical content. Google rewards unique, valuable content.

How to fix it: Take the time to write your own unique product descriptions. Tell a story, highlight the benefits, and use your brand's voice. It's good for SEO and even better for conversions.

"Writing unique descriptions is the single best investment you can make in your product page SEO."

Conclusion

SEO isn't a one-time task, but fixing these common mistakes will build a powerful foundation for sustainable organic growth. By focusing on unique content and a solid technical setup, you put yourself miles ahead of the competition.
